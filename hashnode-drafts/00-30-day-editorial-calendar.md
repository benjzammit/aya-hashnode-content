# AYA 30-Day Hashnode Editorial Calendar

## Goal
Launch with a small but strategically strong content library that defines the category, attracts practical search intent, and builds trust.

## Publishing rhythm
Recommended launch rhythm:
- Week 1: 2 articles
- Week 2: 1 article
- Week 3: 2 articles
- Week 4: 1 article

This keeps quality high and gives time to refine based on early signals.

## CTA pattern
Each article should end with a soft commercial CTA:
- explore whether AYA fits your workflow
- understand where synthetic audiences help and where they do not
- pressure-test messaging, concepts, and campaign ideas earlier

## Review process
For each draft before publish:
- check for generic phrases
- remove any ungrounded claims
- ensure the opening earns the read
- confirm the article links naturally to at least 2 related pieces
- keep the CTA calm and credible

## Month-one objective
By the end of the first 30 days, AYA should have enough content to:
- explain the category clearly
- support homepage credibility
- begin ranking for core definitional and comparison intent
- create a foundation for internal linking and future expansion
